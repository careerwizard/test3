AISSMS College of Engineering

AISSMS College of Engineering All India Shri Shivaji Memorial Society (AISSMS) was established in 1917 by Late H H Shrimant Shri Shahu Chhatrapati Maharaj of Kolhapur to promote the noble cause of education. The AISSMS COE, Pune is a co-education Institute established in 1992. The College of Engineering is affiliated to the University of Pune. It conducts AICTE approved courses leading to the degree of Bachelor of Engineering (BE) in eight engineering streams and Master of Engineering (ME) in Seven engineering streams

It is recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), Pune. It is the first Institute to be accredited with A+ grade by NAAC (3.27 CGPA) in the first cycle. The College within a short span has established itself as one of the best Engineering Colleges in Maharashtra and also in India.

AISSMS College of Engineering received the “Best Professional College” Award in Urban Category by Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), Pune. AISSMS COE offers BE, ME, Ph.D. courses to students applying at the college, admission to which is both merit and entrance based. The cut-off for BE admissions ranges between 95-106. In addition to this, the placement cell of the institute is growing every year. The majority of students placed in the years 2017, 2018, and 2019 are 301, 310, and 325 respectively. The major recruiters of the college are Godrej, IBM, TATA Consultancy Services, L’Oreal, Castrol, ICICI Bank, etc.
