Maharashtra Institute of Technology World peace UniversityMIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune is one of the leading private engineering and management institutions in India which is ranked and recognized by several prestigious national ranking authorities. Recently, the institute has been ranked at the 16th position in the Times of India Rankings 2022 for its MBA program which is offered on the basis of the CAT/MAT/XAT/CMAT/PERACET/MHT-CET entrance scores. Apart from this, the institute is placed at 2nd position for its management programs in the West Zone of India.

MIT-WPU Placements 2021 has been successfully concluded. More than 1000 students got placed. The highest salary recorded was INR 44.14 LPA which is offered by Amazon. The average salary stood at INR 7.25 LPA. MIT WPU Pune has a state-of-the-art campus, students can take a 360 Virtual Tour of the university campus. MITWPU Pune offers degree and doctorate programs in the discipline of Engineering, Pharmacy, Law, Journalism, Management, etc. The university also has an MIT School of Distance Education, Pune offering postgraduate diploma courses in the discipline of Management, Marketing, Finance, etc
