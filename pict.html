Pune Institute of Computer Technology

Pune Institute of Computer Technology, (or PICT) is a private unaided engineering college located in Dhankawadi, Pune, India. It was established by the Society for Computer Technology and Research, SCTR in 1983. It offers degrees in Information Technology, Computer Engineering and Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. PICT was accredited by India's two major accreditation agencies - National Assessment and Accreditation Council [NAAC] and National Board of Accreditation [NBA].

PICT was ranked 8th on the list of best Engineering College of India by the Edu-Rand Rankings in 2016. The survey was done jointly by Edu, an Indian company and Rand Corporation, a non-profit American thinktank.

PICT has recently introduced some new branches like AI-ML. PICT Pune has a dedicated Training & Placement cell which aims at coordinating all the placement operations and supports the recruitment process for the institute. It serves as a common link between the sector and the students. The PICT Pune placement cell offers career guidance and arranges various training sessions for the students of varied aspirations and abilities and with diversified needs. A large number of reputed recruiters from across the country visit the campus to conduct interviews.
